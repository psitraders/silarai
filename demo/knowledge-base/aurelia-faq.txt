AURELIA FINE JEWELLERY — FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Q: Is your gold hallmarked?
A: Yes. All our gold jewellery is BIS Hallmarked, so you are assured of certified purity.

Q: Are the diamonds certified?
A: Diamonds of 0.30 carat and above come with an IGI or SGL certificate of authenticity.

Q: How long does delivery take?
A: Standard delivery is 3–5 business days across India, and 2–3 days for metro cities. You get an SMS and email with a tracking link once your order ships.

Q: Do you offer free shipping?
A: Yes, shipping is free on all orders above Rs. 2,000. Below that, a flat Rs. 99 fee applies.

Q: Can I return a product?
A: Yes, within 7 days of delivery, as long as the item is unworn and comes with its original packaging, tags, and certificate. Customised pieces and pierced jewellery (earrings, nose pins) cannot be returned for hygiene reasons.

Q: How long do refunds take?
A: Refunds are processed within 5–7 business days after the returned item passes our quality check.

Q: Do you offer Cash on Delivery?
A: Yes, COD is available for orders up to Rs. 20,000. There is no limit for prepaid online orders.

Q: Is EMI available?
A: Yes, EMI is offered on orders above Rs. 10,000 through participating banks.

Q: Do you resize rings?
A: Ring resizing is free within 30 days of purchase, one size up or down.

Q: Is there a warranty?
A: We provide a lifetime warranty on manufacturing defects, plus free lifetime cleaning and polishing. Gold plating is guaranteed for 12 months against tarnishing.

Q: How should I care for my jewellery?
A: Store each piece separately, keep it away from perfume and chemicals, and remove it before swimming or bathing.
