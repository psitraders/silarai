# Aurelia Fine Jewellery — Store Policies

## Shipping & Delivery
- We ship across India. Standard delivery takes 3–5 business days; metro cities usually arrive in 2–3 days.
- Orders above ₹2,000 ship free. Below ₹2,000, a flat ₹99 shipping fee applies.
- All jewellery is shipped in tamper-proof, insured packaging. A tracking link is sent by SMS and email once dispatched.
- We currently do not offer international shipping.
- Cash on Delivery (COD) is available for orders up to ₹20,000. Online prepaid orders have no limit.

## Returns & Exchange
- We accept returns and exchanges within 7 days of delivery.
- Items must be unworn, undamaged, and returned with the original packaging, tags, and certificate of authenticity.
- Customised, engraved, or made-to-order pieces cannot be returned or exchanged.
- Pierced jewellery (nose pins, earrings) cannot be returned for hygiene reasons unless it arrived damaged.
- Refunds are processed to the original payment method within 5–7 business days after the returned item passes quality check.
- For COD orders, refunds are issued via bank transfer or UPI.

## Warranty & Quality
- All gold jewellery is BIS Hallmarked. Diamonds above 0.30 carat come with an IGI or SGL certificate.
- We offer a lifetime warranty on manufacturing defects.
- Free cleaning and polishing is available for life at any Aurelia counter.
- Plating (gold-plated / rose-gold) is guaranteed for 12 months against tarnishing under normal use.

## Jewellery Care
- Store each piece separately in a soft pouch to avoid scratches.
- Keep jewellery away from perfume, sanitiser, chlorine, and household chemicals.
- Remove jewellery before swimming, bathing, or sleeping.
- Clean gold and diamond pieces with a soft brush and mild soapy water; pat dry.

## Payments
- We accept UPI, credit/debit cards, net banking, and Cash on Delivery.
- Online payments are processed securely via Razorpay. We never store your card details.
- EMI is available on orders above ₹10,000 through participating banks.

## Customisation
- Made-to-order and custom designs take 10–15 business days.
- Customisation requires a 50% advance, which is non-refundable once production begins.
- Ring resizing is free within 30 days of purchase (one size up or down).

## Privacy
- We collect only the information needed to process your order: name, phone, address, and email.
- We never sell or share your personal data with third parties for marketing.
- Payment information is handled entirely by our payment gateway and is not stored on our servers.
- You can request deletion of your account and data anytime by contacting support.
