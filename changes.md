Left 6 migrations intentionally un-attributed as fully-superseded duplicates: `AddPlatformSettings`, `AddCustomerBirthday`, `AddWhatsAppCatalogId`, `AddPaymentGatewayFields`, `AddAutonomousAi`, `AddB2CB2BFeatures`. See `context.md` §4.6 for the full breakdown and known follow-ups (lost `PlatformSettings` seed row, stale `AppDbContextModelSnapshot.cs`).
